Tickets

The ticket types can be found below. We hope to welcome Girton students, alumni, guests, staff and the wider Cambridge community.

Standard

£125

Standard tickets grant you entry to the what promises to be the best Spring Ball. With a myriad of entertainment for all tastes, Girton Spring Ball is the place to be to see out the end of Lent term.

Queue Jump

£135

Queue jump tickets are the same as standard but allow the ticketholder to enter the ball before standard ticket holders do, giving you more time to party the night away.
