Access Statement

  1. There will be wheelchair access into the ball, the ball will be all on one level with step free access and ramps where necessary. We will try and ensure that as many ents and fairground rides as possible are made accessible.

  2. There will be blue badge holder parking available on site, but this must be pre-booked.

  3. There will be seating available in each area of the ball, and on request in the queue.

  4. There will be an accessible toilet and a gender neutral toilet, both of which will be signposted. There will also be priority toilet access for those with accessibility requirements (e.g. IBS).

  5. There are hearing loops available in all public college rooms.

  6. There will be a designated quiet space.

  7. There will be soft drinks available throughout the ball.

  8. Please get in contact to discuss any food allergies/requirements. We will try to cater to as many dietary requirements as possible.

  9. If you need an Auto Adrenaline Injector, please bring one with you on the night as the college does not keep a stock of these.

  10. If you have any questions about accessibility at GSB2023 you can contact our Accessibility lead via email, - your information will be not be shared, except with the colleges disability liaison officer (and other staff members, with your permission, where this is deemed necessary).
