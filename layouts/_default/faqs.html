Accessibility Information

  1. We are dedicated to making Girton Spring Ball 2024 as accessible as possible! We are engaging with the Cambridge SU Disabled Student’s Campaign’s Access-a-Ball scheme again this year, and strive to maintain our platinum award this year. A full access statement, which contains detailed information on different access needs, will be available on this page soon. We will be providing:

  2. Step-free/wheelchair access to all areas of the ball

  3. Wheelchair accessible, priority access, and non-gendered toilets

  4. Seating available in all areas of the ball, including the Marquee in Woodlands Court

  5. Menu options to cater to dietary requirements

  6. There will be a designated quiet space.

  7. A hearing loop available in all public rooms of the Ball

  8. If you have any concerns or require support in advance of, or on the night of the Ball, please speak to any member of staff or committee, or email gsb24accessibility@gmail.com
